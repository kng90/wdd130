About Me

Hey y'all! I am Kayla Ng

(pronounced like eeng)

traveling around the world and came back home to stay. I have been
married almost 8 years to my husband Scott. We have two incredible

We love to camp, hike, explore, and travel to places we've never
seen before. I really enjoy dancing, baking, and hanging out with

of 2015 with only one semester left to graduate. I ended up working
as a graphic designer, but quickly bored of sitting at a desk all day.
I became a flight attendant for a few years and worked in marketing
until I had my daughter. Now I am lucky enough to be a stay-at-home-mom
and work part-time as a travel agent. I am excited to finally finish up
my degree and get that under my belt. Can't wait to work with all of you
and get to know you!
